The four-phase delivery system

Adapted from the Corey Ganim/Greg Isenberg assessment model, but specialized for healthcare growth and commercialization teams.

1
Discovery

45-minute workflow interview

We map yesterday’s work, dreaded tasks, stuck handoffs, repeated writing, CRM gaps, meeting follow-up, reporting loops, and failed automation attempts.

2
Analysis

AI + human review

We analyze the transcript, identify bottlenecks, research tools, and substitute healthcare-appropriate solutions where generic AI would over-prescribe.

3
Report

Custom prescription

The report includes top pain points, priority matrix, recommended tools, 4-day quick-start plan, and estimated time or revenue impact.

4
Review

30-minute walkthrough

We walk the team through each recommendation, identify the most urgent win, and decide whether they DIY or ask PredictCare to implement.
